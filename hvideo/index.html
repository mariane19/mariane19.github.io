INFORMATION

The original is a boy's manga written by Eiichiro Oda, which is currently being serialized in "Weekly Shonen Jump" (Shueisha). Since its serialization began in 1997, it quickly gained popularity, and in 2021 the comics exceeded 100 volumes. In addition, the total number of copies published in the series has exceeded 500 million (as of 2024).

A TV animation began broadcasting in 1999. Since then, it has become a long series that has always been very popular, and in 2021 it finally exceeded 1,000 episodes. In 2024, it will celebrate its 25th anniversary of broadcasting.

Many theatrical versions have also been produced, and the latest film, "ONE PIECE FILM RED," released in 2022, was a hot topic even before its release, with a total of 14.74 million people attending in Japan and a box office revenue of 20.33 billion yen (including encore screenings in 2023). It was the best-ever result for a theatrical version series.

In 2023, the live-action drama version of "ONE PIECE", with Eiichiro Oda as executive producer, will be exclusively distributed worldwide on Netflix. It has been a big hit both in Japan and overseas, and has won numerous awards. A sequel is already in the works. In addition, numerous media mix works such as games and related products have been created, and related events have been held without interruption to this day. It is a global entertainment work that continues to grow while starting many movements.
